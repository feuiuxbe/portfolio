Major learning challenges

Neither school nor institute teach us how to study the material correctly. We prepare for exams and teach tickets. We practice solving monotonous problems in order to better pass the test, but often in the end it does not give us real knowledge. Cramming quickly wears off and is not useful.

Conclusion: you also need to be able to learn, but for some reason this is taught in few places. What to do about it?

Specific techniques and exercises help transform the way teaching is done, making it effective and exciting. The same techniques are applied on the example of teaching in the Workshop.

Learning principles

by Josh Kaufman

  • 1

    Choose an attractive project.

  • 2

    Focus on one skill.

  • 3

    Determine your target skill level.

  • 4

    Break the skill down into elements.

  • 5

    Prepare everything you need for class.

  • 6

    Remove obstacles to exercise.

  • 7

    Set aside a dedicated time to study.

  • 8

    Create fast feedback loops.

  • 9

    Exercise on a schedule, in short, intense intervals.

  • 10

    Pay attention to quantity and speed.
